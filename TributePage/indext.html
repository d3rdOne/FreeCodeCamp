Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates

Adventure Highlights of Monkey D. Luffy:

  • East Blue Saga - Luffy and Staw Hat Pirates introduction.
    • Luffy meet Shanks Captiain of Red Hair Pirates.
    • Luffy gained his Devil Fruit abilities.
    • Defeated a number of individual(s) or group(s) like Black Cat Pirates, Buggy Pirates, Krieg Pirates, and Arlong Pirates.
    • Gathered his first four nakamas: Zoro, Nami, Usopp, and Sanji. This time luffy have a bounty of 30,000,000 Beli.
  • Alabasta Saga - Straw Hat Pirates entered Grand Line and being targeted by Baroque Works.
    • Met the whale Laboon which is a friend of the Rhumbar Pirates, the former crew of soon to be Straw Hat member, Brook the musician.
    • Obtained a Log Pose from Crocus which can be used to travel on Grand Line.
    • Gained his new crew member Tony Tony Chopper and Nico Robin.
    • Met his brother Portgas D. Ace the princess of Alabasta, Nefeltari Vivi which became his ally.
    • Defeated the Shichibukai, Crocodile.
  • Sky Island Saga - introduction of Skypiea.
    • Discovery of a Sky Island Legend Skypiea. And introduction to the possibility of more sky islands.
    • Defated the Skypiea God .
